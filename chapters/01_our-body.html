Chapter 01

Our Amazing Body 🫀

Your body is like a super machine! Let's find out what is inside you and how it all works together. Ready? Let's go! 🚀

🫀 Let's Discover

Your Super Body

Did you know your body is always working — even when you sleep? 😴

Right now your heart is beating, your lungs are breathing, and your brain is reading these words! All at the same time! 🤯

Your body is made of tiny pieces called cells. Cells are so small you cannot see them! They join together to make your bones, muscles, skin, and everything else. Let's meet the amazing parts of your body! 🌟

🦴

Your Bones — The Strong Frame

You have 206 bones inside your body! They give you your shape — without them you would be all wobbly like a jellyfish! 🪼

🏗️ Bones give you shape — your spine keeps you standing tall!
🛡️ Bones protect you — your skull is like a helmet for your brain!
💪 Bones help you move — when muscles pull on bones, you walk, run, and jump!
🩸 Bones make blood — inside some big bones, new blood is being made right now!

Fun fact: Babies are born with about 270 bones! As you grow up, some of them join together. Cool, right? 😄

💪

Your Muscles — The Movers

You have more than 600 muscles! Muscles are what make you move. Every time you smile, blink, or wiggle your toes — that's your muscles working! 😁

🧠 Your brain tells your muscles what to do by sending tiny signals.
🤝 Muscles work in teams of two — when one pulls, the other rests. Then they swap!
❤️ Your heart is a special muscle that never stops working. It beats all day and all night!

Fun fact: The biggest muscle in your body is in your bottom — it helps you stand up and climb stairs! 🏔️

🫀

Your Heart — The Pumping Machine

Your heart is about the size of your fist. It pumps blood all around your body every single second! 💓

🔴 Blood carries oxygen (the air you breathe in) to every part of your body.
🛡️ Blood also has tiny germ fighters called white blood cells that keep you healthy!
🔄 Blood travels in tubes called blood vessels — they go everywhere, even to your toes!

Fun fact: Your heart beats about 100,000 times every day! Put your hand on your chest — can you feel it? 🥁

🧠

Your Brain — The Boss

Your brain is the boss of your whole body! It sits inside your skull and tells everything what to do. 🎩

🤔 Your brain helps you think, learn, and remember things.
😊 It controls your feelings — like when you feel happy or scared.
⚡ It sends super-fast messages through your body using nerves — like tiny wires!

Fun fact: Your brain is always working, even when you are asleep — it helps you dream! 💤✨

🫁

Your Lungs — The Air Machines

Your lungs are like two big balloons inside your chest. They help you breathe in the air you need to live! 🌬️

inhale Oxygen — the air that keeps your body working.
exhale Carbon Dioxide — the air your body doesn't need anymore.

Fun fact: When you run or play, you breathe faster to get more oxygen! Try taking a deep breath — that's your lungs at work! 🏃‍♀️💨

🧫

Cells — The Building Blocks

Your body is made of tiny pieces called cells. Cells are so small you cannot see them! They join together to make your bones, muscles, skin, and everything else. 🌟

Fun fact: Your body has trillions of cells — more than all the stars in the sky! ✨ Cells have different jobs — some are skin cells, some are blood cells, and some are muscle cells, and even Cells have different shapes!

💭
Think About It!

Right now your body is doing so many things without you even thinking about it — breathing, pumping blood, growing, and even fixing tiny scratches on your skin! Your body is more amazing than any robot ever made! 🤖✨

🫁

Your Lungs — Breathing In and Out

Take a deep breath in… and breathe out! 🌬️ That is your lungs at work! You have two lungs inside your chest. When you breathe in, your lungs fill up with air like balloons. They take the oxygen your body needs and push out a gas called carbon dioxide that your body doesn't need. You breathe about 20,000 times every day — and you don't even have to think about it!

🍽️

Your Tummy — Where Food Goes

When you eat a yummy sandwich 🥪, where does it go? First you chew it with your teeth. Then you swallow and it slides down a tube into your stomach. Your stomach is like a mixing machine — it mashes the food into tiny bits! Then the good stuff (called nutrients) goes into your blood to give you energy. The journey of your food takes about a whole day! 🕐

🫀

Your Heart — Pump, Pump, Pump!

Your heart is a super strong muscle about the size of your fist! ✊ It pumps blood all around your body through tubes called blood vessels. The blood carries oxygen from your lungs to every part of you — your fingers, your toes, and even your brain! Then the blood comes back to your heart and the whole trip starts again. One full trip takes only about one minute! ⏱️

🦴

Your Bones — Strong and Alive!

Did you know your bones are alive? They grow, they get stronger, and they can even fix themselves if they break! 💪 Your bones all connect at places called joints. Some joints let you bend (like your knee 🦵), and some let you twist (like your neck!). Drinking milk 🥛 and eating healthy food helps your bones grow big and strong!

🧠

Your Brain — The Control Centre

Your brain is the smartest part of your body! 🎓 Different parts of the brain do different jobs. One part helps you see, another helps you hear, and another helps you remember your favourite song! 🎵 Your brain also makes sure you keep breathing and your heart keeps beating — even when you're fast asleep! Getting lots of sleep helps your brain learn and grow. 😴💤

🤯 WOW! If you could lay out all your blood vessels (the tiny tubes that carry blood) in a straight line, they would stretch over 96,000 kilometres — that's enough to go around the Earth more than two times! And all of that fits inside your body! 🌍🌍
