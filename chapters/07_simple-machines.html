Chapter 07

Simple Machines ⚙️

Simple machines are amazing helpers! They make hard jobs much easier — and they have been helping people for thousands of years! Let's find out how. 💪🚀

⚙️ Let's Discover

What is a Simple Machine?

Have you ever used a see-saw, a slide, or a wheelbarrow? Then you have already used a simple machine!

A simple machine is a tool that helps you push, pull, or lift things with less effort. It makes hard work easier! There are 6 types of simple machines — and they are everywhere around you! Let's meet them all. ⚙️✨

🪝

The Lever — Push & Lift!

A lever is a long bar that rests on a point in the middle. When you push one end down, the other end goes up! 🎢

🎪 A see-saw in the playground is a lever!
✂️ Scissors are two levers working together!
🥄 Even a spoon can be a lever when you use it to open a lid!

Fun fact: Your own arm is a lever too! Your elbow is the resting point, and your muscles do the pushing! 💪 Lever is a simple machine that helps us lift, push, or pull things with less effort. It helps us to do work easily.

🛞

Wheel & Axle — Roll Along!

A wheel is a round circle that spins around a stick in the middle called an axle. Together they make things roll! 🎡

🚗 Cars have wheels that roll on the road!
🚲 Bicycles use wheels to go fast!
🚪 A door knob is a wheel and axle — you turn it to open the door!

Fun fact: Without wheels, we would have to drag everything on the ground! Imagine dragging your school bag everywhere — no thank you! Wheel and axle is a simple machine that helps us to move things easily. It has two parts: wheel and axle.

🛝

The Ramp — Slide Up Easy!

A ramp is a flat surface that goes up at an angle. Instead of lifting something straight up, you can push it along the ramp — and it's much easier!

🛝 A playground slide is a ramp!
♿ Wheelchair ramps help people get into buildings!
🛹 Skateboard ramps help you go up and do cool tricks!

Fun fact: Long ago, people in Egypt used giant ramps to push heavy stone blocks up to build the pyramids! 🏛️🇪🇬 Ramp is a simple machine that helps us to move things easily. It has two parts: ramp and load.

🏗️

The Pulley — Pull It Up!

A pulley is a wheel with a rope around it. When you pull the rope down, the thing on the other end goes up! It's like magic! 🪄

🏗️ Big cranes use pulleys to lift heavy things!
🏴 Flag poles use pulleys — you pull the rope down and the flag goes up!
🪣 Old water wells used pulleys to pull up buckets of water!

Fun fact: With enough pulleys and rope, even a kid could lift something as heavy as a car! 🚗 Pulley is a simple machine that helps us to move things easily. It has two parts: pulley and rope.

🔩

The Screw — Twist & Hold!

A screw is like a ramp wrapped around a stick! When you turn a screw, it digs in and holds things together really tight! 🔧

🪑 Screws hold your chair and table together!
🫙 A jar lid is a screw — you twist it to open and close!
💡 A light bulb screws into the lamp!

Fun fact: Screws are so strong that they hold together buildings, bridges, and even aeroplanes! ✈️🌉 Screw is a simple machine that helps us to move things easily. It has two parts: screw and rope.

🪓

The Wedge — Split It Apart!

A wedge is a piece that is thick on one end and thin on the other. You push the thin end into something and it splits it apart! 💥

🪓 An axe is a wedge — it splits wood!
🔪 A knife is a wedge — it cuts food!
🦷 Your front teeth are wedges — they bite into your food!

Fun fact: Even a door stopper is a wedge! You push the thin end under the door to keep it open! 🚪✨ Wedge is a simple machine that helps us to move things easily. It has two parts: wedge and rope.

💭
Think About It!

Long, long ago, the people of ancient Egypt 🇪🇬 built the amazing pyramids using simple machines! They used ramps to push heavy stone blocks up, and levers to lift them into place. They had no cars, no electricity, and no robots — just simple machines and lots of teamwork! 🏛️💪

🪝

Levers Are Everywhere!

A lever has three parts: the bar (the long part), the resting point (where it balances), and the load (what you are lifting). 🎯 When you push down on one side, the other side goes up — like a see-saw! The longer the bar, the easier it is to lift heavy things. That's why it's easy to open a paint tin with a long screwdriver but hard with a short one! 🪛 Lever can be used to lift, push, or pull things. Lever has three parts: the bar (the long part), the resting point (where it balances), and the load (what you are lifting). Lever is a simple machine that helps us to move things easily. The first lever was invented by Archimedes in ancient Greece. And it was used to lift heavy stones to build pyramids!

🛞

Wheels Changed the World!

Before wheels were invented, people had to carry everything or drag it on the ground! Then someone had the brilliant idea to put round circles under things — and everything became easier! 🎉 Today wheels are on cars, buses, trains, bicycles, skateboards, suitcases, and even shopping trolleys! Wheels help things move smoothly with much less effort. 🛒 The first wheel was invented about 5500 years ago in Mesopotamia. And it was not used for transport but for pottery! 🏺

🛝

Ramps Help Everyone!

Carrying a heavy box up stairs is really hard! But pushing it up a ramp is much easier! That's because a ramp spreads the work over a longer distance. This is why buildings have ramps for wheelchairs and prams, why car parks have ramps to drive up, and why delivery workers use ramps to load big boxes into trucks! Ramps help everyone! ♿🚚📦 The first ramp was used to build the pyramids in ancient Egypt! And it was used to lift heavy stones to build pyramids!

🏗️

Pulleys Are Super Helpers!

Have you ever raised a flag on a flag pole? 🏴 You pull the rope down and the flag goes up! That's a pulley at work! Pulleys are used in cranes to build tall buildings 🏗️, in lifts to carry people up and down, and even on sailing boats to pull up the heavy sails! ⛵ The more pulleys you use together, the easier it is to lift really heavy things! The first pulley was invented by Archimedes in ancient Greece. And it was very useful for lifting heavy stones to build temples and palaces!

🔩

Screws & Wedges at Home!

Look around your home — screws and wedges are everywhere! 🏠 The screws in your furniture hold everything together. The lid on your water bottle is a screw — twist it to open! 🫙 Your knife is a wedge that cuts food, your front teeth are wedges that bite, and even a nail is a wedge that pushes into wood! Screws are used to fix things together. And wedges helps to cut things. There are many types of screws and wedges. We use screws and wedges in our daily lives. And they are very useful for us.

🤯 WOW! Your body is full of simple machines! Your arm is a lever, your teeth are wedges, and your elbow works like a hinge! Even when you open a door or climb stairs, you are using simple machines. You are basically a walking, talking machine!✨
