Chapter 10

Saving Our Planet 🌍

Our planet is in trouble — but together, we can make it better! Learn what YOU can do to help save the Earth.

🌍 Let's Discover

Why Does Our Planet Need Help?

Human activities have been harming Earth's air, water, land and living creatures. But science gives us the tools to fix these problems — and every small action counts!

🌡️

Global Warming

The Earth's average temperature is rising. Burning fossil fuels (coal, oil, gas) releases carbon dioxide (CO2) into the atmosphere. CO2 traps the Sun's heat like a blanket — this is the greenhouse effect, and it's causing global warming and climate change.

🌲

Deforestation

Trees are being cut down at an alarming rate — for farmland, timber and cities. Forests are home to 80% of all land species! Trees also absorb CO2 and produce oxygen. Losing forests accelerates climate change and destroys habitats.

🗑️

Pollution

Pollution contaminates air, water and soil. Factories release toxic gases. Plastic waste fills oceans — over 8 million tonnes end year! Oil spills kill marine life. Pesticides harm insects and birds. Pollution affects all living things, including humans.

🦏

Endangered Species

Human activities are causing animals and plants to go extinct 1,000 times faster than natural rates. Habitat destruction, hunting, pollution and climate change threaten millions of species. Losing a species means losing it forever — extinction is permanent.

💭
Think About It!

You are not too small to make a difference! Greta Thunberg started her climate strike at age 15 and changed the world's conversation about climate change. Scientists, inventors, and ordinary people of all ages are working to save our planet. What will YOU do? 🌱

Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

Reduce: Buy less, use less. Reuse: Use things again before throwing them away. Recycle: Turn waste into new products. Recycling one aluminium can saves enough energy to run a TV for 3 hours! If everyone reduced waste by just 10%, it would make an enormous difference.

🌱

Planting Trees

Trees absorb CO2, produce oxygen, prevent soil erosion, provide habitat, and cool the air. The Trillion Tree Campaign aims to plant a trillion trees worldwide — and YOU can be part of it! Even planting one tree in your garden makes a difference for birds and insects.

☀️

Clean Energy

Switching from fossil fuels to renewable energy (solar, wind, hydro) is the most impactful solution to climate change. Solar panels on rooftops generate clean electricity. Electric vehicles powered by renewable energy produce zero emissions. Clean energy creates more jobs than fossil fuels!

🌊

Protecting Oceans

Oceans cover 71% of Earth and absorb 90% of excess heat from global warming. We can protect them by reducing plastic use, supporting marine protected areas, eating sustainable seafood, and cleaning up beaches. Healthy oceans mean a healthy planet!

🐝

Protecting Biodiversity

Biodiversity means the variety of life on Earth — and it's the key to healthy ecosystems. We can protect it by preserving habitats, reducing pesticide use, creating wildlife corridors, and supporting conservation organisations. Every species plays a role — even bees and earthworms!

🤯 WOW! Nature is incredibly resilient! The ozone layer — which protects us from harmful UV radiation — was being destroyed by chemicals called CFCs. When countries agreed to ban CFCs in 1987 (the Montreal Protocol), the ozone layer started healing. It is now expected to fully recover by 2066. When humans work together, we CAN fix environmental problems!
